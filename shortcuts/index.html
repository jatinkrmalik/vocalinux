Quick Reference

Keyboard Shortcuts & Voice Commands

Master Vocalinux with keyboard shortcuts and voice commands. Use toggle mode (double-tap) or push-to-talk mode, then apply voice commands for editing and formatting.

Quick Start

Ctrl+Ctrl
→Toggle Mode: Start/Stop Dictation

Push-to-talk mode is also available: hold your configured key to dictate, release to stop. You can switch modes in Settings.

Voice commands can be enabled or disabled from Settings depending on your workflow.
